Rice cooker Fried Rice

Easy Fried Rice that will make your mama proud!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Jasmine rice
  • 1 ¼ cup chicken broth
  • ¾ cup frozen vegetables
  • ¾ cups Chinese sausage, cut into ½" thick rounds, then halved 2 links
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup onion, chopped
  • 1 egg, lightly whisked
  • ½ tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon sugar
  • green onion for garnish

Directions

  1. Prep the ingredients, wash and drain the rice, mince the garlic, chop up the onion and cut the sausage into smaller pieces.
    2. Add the rice, broth, garlic, onion, fish sauce, soy sauce, sugar and butter. Then give it a swirl to mix together.
  2. Add veggies, sausage, and a beaten egg on top of the water.
  3. Set the rice cooker to cook. When done, fluff the rice and mix the ingredients together.
