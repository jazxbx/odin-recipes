Ingredients

    Ingredients below are for a 5.5-cup cooker. Double the ingredients for a 10-cup cooker.
  • 50g All-purpose flour or pancake flour
  • 40g Pure cocoa powder
  • 5g Baking powder
  • 50g Sugar
  • Large egg, whisked
  • 1 cup Milk or unsweetened oat milk
  • Cooking spray

Directions

  1. Sift the flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the sugar and stir.
  3. Add the egg and milk and stir until the batter is smooth.
  4. Spray the rice cooker inner pot with cooking spray and pour the batter in.
  5. Select the [Slow Cook] function and set the timer to 40 minutes. Press start.
